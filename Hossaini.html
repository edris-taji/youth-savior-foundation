About Mr.Hossaini

Who Am I?

Sayed Habibullah Hossaini was born in 1994 in a science-loving and culture-loving family in Herat, Afghanistan. In 2013, he successfully graduated from Ostad Riaz High School in Herat and started the Faculty of Law and Political Sciences, Department of Administrative & Diplomacy at Jami University.

After graduated from faculty of Law and Political Science, Hossaini got the idea of establishing a social institution to struggle for Human Rights, Women Identity, Women Empowerment, Afghan youth capacity building, liberal studies meetings and seminars for afghan youths. So he with a commited team of other civil activists, founded the none profit organization of “Youth Savior Socail Foundation”.

In this Organization Hossaini have done a great deal of voluntary works like; teaching orphans and street children, running conferences for women’s rights, introduced many students to universities and help them with their university tuitions, ran so many advocacy motives for both children and women. Hossaini, also founded two other none profit organization “Hemat Learning Center and Naji Learning Center: in which he taught orphans and street children for free and provide them learning staff.

Besides that Hossaini run many advocacy to protest violence against women and also hold many seminars about women and children rights, citizenship’s rights and reponsibilty, management and promoting democracy in Afghanistan. Furthermore, he founded the ASAS (American System for Afghan Students) Academy and taught English for a long time. In 2022 he got 83 on the TOEFL ibt exam. He was also a University lecturer for a time and teaching English in several private universities and institutes.
