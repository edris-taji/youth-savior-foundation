Our Vision and Mission

Our Vision

The vision for Youth Savior Foundation is to create a space that is open and welcoming to everyone in the community, regardless of their background or socio-economic status. We aim to foster a sense of belonging and civic pride, and to promote social cohesion and a commitment to the common good. To achieve this vision, we strive to offer a wide range of programs and services that are responsive to the needs and interests of the community.

These may include cultural programs, educational initiatives, recreational activities, community events, and opportunities for civic engagement. We place a strong emphasis on transparency and accountability, and we are dedicated to being a model of good governance. We work closely with community members to ensure that our policies and procedures are clear and equitable, and we are committed to involving stakeholders in decision-making processes.

Ultimately, our goal is to create a space where everyone feels welcome, respected, and valued, and where people can come together to build a stronger and more connected community. We are committed to making a positive difference in the lives of those we serve, and to helping to create a more just and equitable society for all.

Our Mission

The mission of Youth Savior Social Foundation is to create a welcoming and inclusive space that serves the diverse needs and interests of the community. We aim to promote civic engagement, social cohesion, and a sense of responsibility towards the common good. To achieve our mission, we provide a range of programs and services that are responsive to the needs and aspirations of the community. These include cultural programs, educational opportunities, recreational activities, community events, and civic initiatives.

We are committed to transparency and accountability, and to being a model of good governance. We involve community members in decision-making processes, establish clear policies and procedures, and are accountable to stakeholders for the outcomes of our work. We believe that by providing a space where people of all backgrounds can find common ground, we can help to build a stronger and more connected community.

We are dedicated to creating a space where everyone feels welcome, respected, and valued, and where everyone has the opportunity to make a positive contribution to the community. Furthermore, Youth Savior Socio-Cultural Foundation aim to create a society where youth are empowered and educated, and where women and children's rights are respected and protected.

The foundation strives to promote social and cultural awareness, civic responsibility, and environmental sustainability in the community through, Education, Health, Cultural, Research, Publication, Youth, Social, Environment, Arts, Communication and Sports Committees. The foundation aims to provide educational opportunities and capacity building classes for people especially women and children.
